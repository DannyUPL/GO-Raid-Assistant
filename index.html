How to use me

A Site for the elite pokemon gamers like me and you..!

This Website designed with love and care as a way of helping guys like you out there looking for a way to get the upper hand in raids and better loot. Created as a college group project made by literal gamers who got together to design a way of choosing which pokemon out of your collection would be best used in any given raid your about to battle. This giving you little help in hand in how well you will do.

Pokemon database Page

Enter your current best pokemon to store for our algorithms to desichper the best of the best

start of by entering the name of the pokemon you wish to add. An image will show up on the left verifying your choice and if your "close but no cigar!" a drop down box will give you some pokemon with similar characters. next add the level and nickname incase you have more of one type. Lastly select the moves your pokemon pocesses from the dropdown boxes press the add button and it will be in your database ready to pick from. A gallery of pokemon you have entered will be presented below and should you wish to edit that collection hover your mouse over the image an edit icon will apear and then you can change level attacks or simple delete all together.
